# Waxworm Infographic

An interactive infographic exploring how waxworm genetics may help solve plastic pollution.

## About This Project

This infographic was created as part of a science communication project to share research findings with a general audience, including beekeepers who contributed waxworm samples from across North Carolina.

## What You'll Learn

- The global plastic pollution problem
- How waxworms can break down polyethylene plastic
- What genome annotation is and why it matters
- Our research process and findings
- Future directions for this research

## Key Research

This work builds on recent discoveries about *Galleria mellonella* (the greater wax moth) and its ability to degrade plastic through enzymes in its saliva.
